My Decision-Making Philosophy

1

Question First, Data Second

I start by defining the business question, not by exploring the data. A clear objective is the foundation for any meaningful analysis.

2

Simplicity Over Complexity

A simple, understandable model that is used is always better than a complex, perfect model that is ignored. Clarity is key.

3

Action is the Goal

My work isn't finished until the insights have been translated into a concrete business action. The final deliverable is a decision, not a report.
